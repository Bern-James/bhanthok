Welcome to Akobo Secondary School

Brief History

Akobo Secondary School is a Christian school based on the Seventh-Day Adventist Foundation and in this, we train students to be good Christians by helping others and also having Faith in God.
The School has managed to almost pass all students with flying colors. Most of our students pass with First Grade with the least in Second Grade. But not only that, we straggle to make even those from other poor school have the best grades before they leave for their National Exams due to the great strong foundation of English speaking and having God as the first priority.
By so, the school has also had a number of activities like drama, debates to mention but a few; which makes the students practice and also learn vocabulary from others.
